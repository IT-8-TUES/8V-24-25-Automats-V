Welcome to Astra Nova

Here you will find comprehensive information about the Universe, its wonders and mysteries. Astra Nova is created with people of all ages in mind and aims to make space knowledge accessible and inspiring.

Planets and Stars

Discover the structure and diversity of our Solar System — from the rocky inner planets to the gas giants and icy worlds beyond. Learn about fascinating exoplanets orbiting distant stars, and explore the life cycles of stars: from birth in nebula to supernova, black holes, and neutron stars.

Space Missions

Explore the most iconic space missions in human history. From the groundbreaking Apollo moon landings and the far-reaching Voyager probes, to modern observatories like the James Webb Space Telescope, uncover how these missions have expanded our understanding of the cosmos and our place in it.
